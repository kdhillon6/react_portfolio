What you get

We send high-resolution JPEG files sized at 8 inches. These are perfect for most applications. If you plan on using your logo large such as a trade show display or a special application like embroidery, be sure to select our vector format option.

Standard purchase

  • 1 customized JPG file with your company name at 2400 x 2400 at 300dpi (8 inches)
  • 1 customized PNG file with your company name at 2400 x 2400 at 300dpi (8 inches)
  • 1 PNG file of your logo only (no type) at 2400 x 2400 at 300dpi (8 inches)
  • 1 thank you email which includes
    • link to the font we have paired with their logo
    • hex numbers for brand colors

Additional Vector purchase

  • 1 customized EPS file with outlined type
